Hi, I'm Carl

My Journey

My journey into the world of technology likely began with my old Windows XP computer. It was slow back then, and it still is, but I make sure it's still working from time to time. After landing my first full-time job in 2019 in technical support, I realised I wanted to dive deeper into the field and figure out what programming was about. That's when I decided to pursue a bachelor's degree in computer science.

What I Do

I'm currently writing my master's thesis in computer science at UiO in collaboration with SINTEF. My topic of choice is within the realm of AI, with specialization in LLMs. Read more about my thesis here.

Who I Am

25-year-old with a passion for playing tennis in the summer, skiing in the winter, hiking, traveling, and going on adventures with friends. I love watching films and have recently started reading books more often, so I'm open to recommendations. While I enjoy all these activities, my secret dream is probably to become a drummer one day—though I believe everything happens in its own time.
